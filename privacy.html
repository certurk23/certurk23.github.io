Privacy Policy

QUANT_MEDIA · quantmedia.io · Effective: March 1, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how QUANT_MEDIA ("we", "our", or "the Site"), operated at quantmedia.io, collects, uses, and shares information when you visit our website. By using this Site, you agree to the practices described below.

All content on QUANT_MEDIA is provided for educational and informational purposes only and does not constitute financial advice.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect personally identifiable information directly. However, the following data may be collected automatically through third-party services:

  • IP address and approximate geographic location (city/country level)
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited, time spent, and navigation behavior
  • Referring website or search query
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)
  • Screen resolution and language settings

3. Cookies

This Site uses cookies — small text files stored on your device — for the following purposes:

  • Analytics cookies — to understand how visitors use the Site (via Google Analytics)
  • Advertising cookies — to serve relevant ads and measure ad performance (via Google AdSense)
  • Preference cookies — to remember your display preferences (e.g., dark/light theme)

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect site functionality.

4. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to collect anonymized usage statistics. Google Analytics uses cookies to track visitor interactions. The data is processed by Google LLC and subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

  • IP anonymization is enabled — full IP addresses are not stored
  • Bot and crawler traffic is filtered from analytics data
  • We do not share analytics data with third parties beyond Google

You may opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

5. Google AdSense

This Site may display advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google uses cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on prior visits to this and other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the internet.

  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings
  • You may also opt out via www.aboutads.info
  • Google AdSense is governed by Google's Privacy Policy

6. Third-Party Market Data

This Site retrieves live market data from the following third-party APIs. These services may log your IP address as part of standard server operations:

  • Finnhub — stock and index quotes (Privacy Policy)
  • CoinGecko — cryptocurrency prices (Privacy Policy)
  • Open Exchange Rates API (open.er-api.com) — foreign exchange rates
  • TradingView — embedded ticker widgets (Privacy Policy)

We do not control the data practices of these third-party services.

7. Data Retention

We do not maintain our own database of user information. Analytics data retained by Google Analytics is subject to Google's standard retention periods (default: 14 months for user-level data). You may request data deletion directly through Google's tools.

8. Children's Privacy

This Site is intended for adults and is not directed at individuals under the age of 13 (or 16 where applicable under GDPR). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — the right to know what data is collected about you
  • Deletion — the right to request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out — the right to opt out of data collection for advertising purposes
  • Portability — the right to receive your data in a structured format (where applicable)

For data collected by Google Analytics or Google AdSense, please exercise these rights directly through Google Account settings.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the Site after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
